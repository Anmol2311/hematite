Introduction

What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing encompasses all marketing efforts that use an electronic device or the internet. In simple terms, any form of marketing that exists online is called as Digital Marketing. Digital Marketing process broadly involves the following steps:

  • Research
  • Plan and Strategize
  • Implement
  • Measure and Audit
  • Optimize
Objective
  • An improved conversion rate.
  • Greater Brand Awareness.
  • An increase in visits to your website.
  • A greater market share.
