About

Hematite Infotech (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. is an organization dedicated to the spread of IT Education. Ever since its inception in 2017, the Academy has fulfilled its goals of providing Quality Computer Education, transforming the aspiring students into tomorrow’s IT professionals. Hematite Infotech has continuously upgraded its performance, creating a focus on advanced information technologies, high-end academics and training.

The center is having modern infrastructure including corporate standard laboratories equipped with latest state of art technologies. We are proud to remain associated with the best faculty in the industry. Besides lectures from expert, visiting faculty, we also organize vide based sessions, seminars, assignments, internship, projects, faculty-student discussions, expert talk and profiling of the students, guidance in Hackathon’s, Start-up’s etc. We also have a well-equipped library, with a wide array of manuals. We also provide the placement assistance to our students. Hematite Infotech has enviable placement record since its inception.

Our solutions include:
  • Customised Training for Corporates
  • Internship Programmes
  • Online Learning
  • Skills Assessments
  • On-Premise Training Delivery
  • Weekdays & Weekends Class
  • Short Skills Development Programmes
